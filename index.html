About

The Green Fellowship is an annual fund donated by UC Davis alum, faculty and community members that supports student projects exploring student-led advancement of social justice, sustainable technology, and environmental sustainability at UC Davis.

Each year, two Green Fellows are selected. Each fellow receives $4,000 and up to $2,500 to be used as directed by the advisor to the project for mentorship support or supplies in support of their projects.

This site showcases the projects of Green Fellows throughout the years
